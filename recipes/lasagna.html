Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the definition of comfort food: saucy, meaty, creamy, delicious.

The word Lasagna comes from the Latin word for cooking pot; so even in antiquity, one-pot meals were staples.

Ingredients

1 Box, Lasagna Pasta

Tomato Sauce

1 Can, Whole Tomatoes

2 Tbps., Olive Oil

2 Cloves, Garlic

Salt to Taste

Cheese Filling

1 Cup, Ricotta Cheese

1/2 Cup, Parmesean Cheese

1 Clove, Garlic

1/2 Tbps., Salt

2 Tsp., Sugar

Meat Fillings

1 Pound, Sweet Italian Sausage

1 Tbps., Italian Seasoning

1/4 Tbps., Sage

Steps

Tomato Sauce

  1. Add all ingredients to a pot
  2. Simmer sauce, stirring occasionally until time for assembly

Cheese Filling

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl
  2. Stir together and sit aside for assembly

Meat Filling

  1. Saute and break up sausage in a pan over medium heat until sausage just develops a crust
  2. Add seasonings and take off heat
  3. Add meat to simmering tomato sauce, leave until assembly

Assembly

  1. Soak pasta in a bowl of lukewarm water, let sit until pasta is pliable
  2. Line the bottom of a casserole dish with a light layer of tomato sauce
  3. Add a layer of pasta
  4. Add a layer of sauce
  5. Add a layer of pasta
  6. Layer in the cheese filling
  7. Layer on pasta
  8. Repeat process until finished, top with sauce and remaining parmesean cheese
  9. Place in an oven preheated to 350 Farenheit for 20 minutes or until cheese topping is melted and browning.
  10. Remove from oven and allow to rest for 5 minutes

Serve
