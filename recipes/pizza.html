Pizza

Description

Pizza is a baked wheat bread with toppings endemic to the northern Italian countryside, notably from the Naples region.

This particular recipe is all about making the crust by hand. The sauce is simple and can be used in nearly any tomato sauce application. Like with any recipe, with toppings, the sky is the limit.

Ingredients

Sauce

1 Can, Whole Tomatoes

1 Tbps., Italian Seasoning

1 Tsp., Olive Oil

Crust

2 and 2/3 Cups, Bread Flour(AP will work in a pinch, just requires more kneading)

3/4 Cup, Water

1 Packet Instant Yeast

2 Tps., Extra Virgin Oil

1 and 1/2 Tbps., Sugar

3/4 Tps., Salt

Suggested Toppings

Buffalo Mozzerella Cheese

Fresh Basil Leaves

Pepperoni

Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

Sauce

  1. Add all ingredients for sauce into a pot over medium heat
  2. Stir consistently for 5 to 10 minutes, add salt to taste

Crust

  1. Add all ingredients into a mixing bowl
  2. Stir ingredients until a workable dough forms
  3. Flour counter and proceed to knead dough for 15 minutes
  4. Place dough in an oiled mxing bowl and cover for an hour to proof
  5. Punch down dough and stretch dough to size

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven to as high of a temperature as possible
  2. Stretch pizza dough to the size of a pan of choice
  3. Add a thin layer of sauce to dough
  4. Add cheese to pizza
  5. Place pizza into oven, monitor periodically until crust is golden brown and crispy
  6. Take pizza out of oven and place rest of toppings on it, drizzle with extra virgin olive oil

Serve
