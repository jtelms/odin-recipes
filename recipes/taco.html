Taco

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican food consisting of a tortilla, any variety of fillings, and toppings.

This recipe takes advantage of that by using modern technology to take the effort out of traditional food.

Ingredients

Tortillas

Filling

1lb., Ground Turkey

1/2 Can, Salsa

1 Tbps., Garlic Powder

1 Tbps., Chili Powder

1/2 Tbps., Smoked Paprika

1/4 Tbps., Salt

Toppings(Optional)

Cilantro

Sour Cream(or Crema)

Diced or Pickled Onions

Shredded Cheese(Preferrably a Mexican Cheese like Oaxaca)

Steps

Filling

  1. Cook ground turkey and spices in a pan over medium heat until turkey begins to form a crust and spices are fragrant
  2. Place ground turkey, spices, and salsa in a slow cooker with the lid on, set to low, for 20 to 35 minutes.

Assembly

  1. Place a portion of filling in a tortilla
  2. Top filling with toppings of choice
  3. Serve
